The Creative Technology class allowed me to take the space and time to learn and experiment with new technology. I'm excited to have had 10 design challenges explore a completely new and innovative side of technology.

Final Design Challenge - Gratitude Card

About: This project is called Verus. It means authentic in Latin. This project is all about mixing the physical and the digital. I created physical card to write and ponder about gratitude thoughts and feelings. I also created that card digitally and made it so you can save it in firebase and email the link of your created image to someone.

Github:https://github.com/Oceanestars/Creative-Technology/tree/main/Verus
